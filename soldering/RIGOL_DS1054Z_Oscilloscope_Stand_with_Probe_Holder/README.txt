# Summary

Table stand for the RIGOL DS1054Z oscilloscope with probe holders. The stand will raise the oscilloscope by 130mm to make more room on your crowded work table, and the screens won't be blocked by multi-meter or the soldering station any more. There are also build in scope holders on the stand for better organization and wire management. 

Should fit to other models in the DS1000 series as well, but I did not test.
