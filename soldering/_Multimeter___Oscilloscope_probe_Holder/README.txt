# Summary

This is a remix of a probe / oscilloscope holder designed to hold the probes for the Rigol DS series and 2 extra slots for a Fluke multimeter.

Modified by [Doctor Bit's Lab](http://www.drbit.nl)



# Print Settings

Rafts: Doesn't Matter
Supports: Doesn't Matter
Resolution: Fast
Infill: 10% or more

Notes: 
My initial print is on automatic and fast options on the Cura software. Recommended to print on ABS to give extra strength. The clip force is quite hard and the plastic needs to be strong.

If you do print on ABS be sure you put heated bed at 100C minimum , recommended at 105C or 110C
